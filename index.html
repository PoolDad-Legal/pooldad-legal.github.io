Key Features

  • 🔄 LIVE Overlay Replies (< 2 s) – GPT‑powered answers appear as lower‑third or TTS while you stream.
  • 💬 Auto‑Comments on Published Videos – PoolDad posts witty replies under your own clips using comment.list + comment.create scopes.
  • 🚀 Road‑map: Direct LIVE‑Chat Posting – will switch on automatically once TikTok releases the write API.
  • 📊 Basic analytics: comment count, median response‑time, top emojis.
  • ⚙️ Easy setup: import a one‑file n8n workflow, drop in keys, done.

Get Started in Minutes

  1. Create a TikTok Developer app and copy your client_key & client_secret.
  2. Add /tiktok/oauth/callback as an OAuth Redirect URI.
  3. Open n8n, paste your OpenAI & TikTok credentials, then import the PoolDad workflow.
  4. Grant Permissions – approve comment.list + comment.create so PoolDad can post under your videos. We’ll request the LIVE‑comment scope automatically once TikTok exposes it.
  5. Activate the workflow — you’re live! 🎉

Automated comments on your own videos are active; LIVE‑chat posting will enable the moment TikTok unlocks the API.
