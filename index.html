Get Started in Minutes

  1. Create your TikTok Developer app and grab your client_key & client_secret.
  2. Define your OAuth Redirect URI (/tiktok/oauth/callback).
  3. Open n8n, configure your OpenAI & TikTok credentials, and import the PoolDad workflow.
  4. Activate the workflow—you’re live! 🎉
