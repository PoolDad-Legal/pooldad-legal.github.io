Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to PoolDad AI Sync (“we”, “us”, “our”). We offer an AI-powered integration that reads TikTok Live comments and returns automated responses via OpenAI. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how and why we use it, how long we keep it, and your rights.

2. Definitions

Service
The PoolDad AI Sync platform integrating TikTok Live with AI-generated replies.
Personal Data
Any information relating to an identifiable person, e.g. TikTok user ID, comment text.
Processing
Any operation performed on Personal Data (collecting, storing, using, deleting).
Controller
PoolDad-Legal, the entity determining purposes and means of processing Personal Data.
Processor
Third-party services (e.g. OpenAI, cloud host) that process data on our behalf.

3. Information We Collect

  • TikTok Profile Data: Username, user ID, locale.
  • Live Comment Data: Text of comments you authorize us to process.
  • Metadata: Timestamps, comment IDs, session identifiers.
  • Usage Logs: API request logs, performance metrics, error traces.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Response Generation: We forward comments to OpenAI to create replies, then post back to TikTok Live.
  • Analytics & Reporting: We aggregate metrics (number of comments processed, response times) to monitor and improve the Service.
  • Support & Troubleshooting: We review logs and data to diagnose and fix issues.
  • Security & Abuse Prevention: We analyze patterns to detect misuse or fraud.
  • Legal Compliance: We use data to respond to legal requests or obligations.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA Users)

  • Consent: You authorize us to process your comment data by logging in.
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is required to provide the Service.
  • Legitimate Interests: To secure, optimize, and analyze the Service.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your Personal Data only as long as necessary—typically up to 30 days. After this, data is permanently deleted. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us at nikolasvergara@icloud.com.

7. Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256 where supported).
  • Access controls restricting data to authorized systems and personnel.
  • Regular security audits, vulnerability scans, and patch management.
  • Secure storage of API keys via environment variables and secret management.

8. Third-Party Processors

We engage these processors under data-processing agreements:

  • OpenAI, Inc.: Generates AI responses based on comment text.
  • Cloud Hosting: Hosts servers/databases under strict security policies.
  • Analytics Tools (e.g. Google Analytics): Collects anonymous usage data.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own, including the U.S. We use standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards to protect your rights.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

11. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your Personal Data.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your data.
  • Objection: Object to processing under certain circumstances.
  • Portability: Obtain your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any right, email us at nikolasvergara@icloud.com.
