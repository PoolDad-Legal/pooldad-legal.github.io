Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to PoolDad AI Sync ("we", "us", "our"). We provide an AI‑powered integration that listens to TikTok LIVE comments, generates witty replies via OpenAI, shows them on‑screen during the broadcast, and—where permitted—posts them directly under your own published videos through TikTok’s comment API. Direct write‑back into LIVE chat will be enabled automatically once TikTok releases the corresponding scope.

2. Definitions

Service
The PoolDad AI Sync platform that connects TikTok with AI‑generated replies.
Personal Data
Information relating to an identifiable person, e.g. TikTok user ID, comment text.
Processing
Any operation performed on Personal Data (collecting, storing, using, deleting).
Controller
PoolDad‑Legal, the entity determining purposes and means of processing Personal Data.
Processor
Third‑party services (e.g. OpenAI, cloud host) that process data on our behalf.

3. Information We Collect

  • TikTok Profile Data: Username, user ID, locale.
  • Comment Data: Text of comments you authorize us to process (LIVE or VOD).
  • Metadata: Timestamps, comment IDs, session identifiers.
  • Usage Logs: API request logs, performance metrics, error traces.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Response Generation: Viewer comments are forwarded to OpenAI to craft a reply; the reply is (a) overlaid on‑screen during LIVE, and (b) posted to the creator’s own video comment thread via comment.create. Posting directly into LIVE chat will begin once TikTok exposes the required write scope.
  • Analytics & Reporting: We aggregate metrics ( comment count, response time ) to improve the Service.
  • Support & Troubleshooting: Logs help diagnose and fix issues.
  • Security & Abuse Prevention: Patterns are analysed to detect misuse.
  • Legal Compliance: Data may be used to respond to legal requests.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA Users)

  • Consent: You authorise processing by logging in via TikTok OAuth.
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is required to provide the Service.
  • Legitimate Interests: To secure, optimise, and analyse the Service.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain Personal Data only as long as necessary—typically up to 30 days. After that period, data is permanently deleted unless a shorter deletion is requested via nikolasvergara@icloud.com.

7. Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest (AES‑256 where supported).
  • Role‑based access controls limiting data to authorised personnel.
  • Regular security audits, vulnerability scans, and patch management.
  • Secure storage of API keys via environment variables and secret management.

8. Third‑Party Processors

We engage these processors under GDPR‑compliant data‑processing agreements:

  • OpenAI, Inc. – generates AI responses.
  • Cloud Hosting Provider – hosts servers/databases.
  • Analytics Tool (e.g. Plausible) – collects anonymous usage data.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, including the U.S. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards to protect your rights.

10. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

To exercise any right, email us at nikolasvergara@icloud.com.
